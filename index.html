Our Program

Fit28 is an ever-changing small group HIIT workout program that combines strength training, cardiovascular training, and nutrition to ensure results! We use technology to ensure our members are in the best position to succeed, and results are tracked in and out of the studio. Our workout theme changes weekly, and exercises change daily to ensure Fit28 members never hit plateaus and are always progressing forward on their fitness journey.

Coach to Athlete

We challenge our members to achieve their very best with high intensity intervals mixed with recovery periods for a “True HIIT” experience. No more long periods spent on the treadmill, because that is not what the science supports when it comes to transformation. Short bursts of intense and focused work followed by recovery periods is what the research supports and that is what we have out clients do! And we track it using the latest heart rate technology that gets saved in your personal account, so you can see your real results over time! Cardio equipment our program uses:
