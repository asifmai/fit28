THE MOST AMAZING 28-MINUTE WORKOUT ON THE MARKET!

Fit28 uses various forms of high intensity interval training and equipment to deliver themost incredible 28-minute functional HIIT workout on the market!Our trainer coached,technology tracked, small group workouts are designed to pull the best, mentally and physically, out of our clients.

our program

Fit28 is an ever-changing small group HIIT workout program that combines strength training, cardiovascular training, and nutrition to ensure results! We use technology to ensure our members are in the best position to succeed, and results are tracked in and out of the studio. Our workout theme changes weekly, and exercises change daily to ensure Fit28 members never hit plateaus and are always progressing forward on their fitness journey.

coached workout

Since we are exercise physiologists, certified exercise professionals, and coaches, Fit28 uses a variety of equipment, rep & set counts, ranges of motion, and planes of motion to challenge our members’ bodies. COACH TO ATHLETE

We challenge our members to achieve their very best with high intensity intervals mixed with recovery periods for a “True HIIT” experience. No more long periods spent on the treadmill, because that is not what the science supports when it comes to transformation. Short bursts of intense and focused work followed by recovery periods is what the research supports and that is what we have out clients do!
